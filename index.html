Help Center

FAQ

How do I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. Use it on our website or the courier's website to track your delivery in real time.
What is your return policy?
We offer 7-day easy returns on all items. Products must be unworn, unwashed, and with original tags attached. Contact us at hello@yourwonderland.com to initiate a return.
How do I find my size?
Each product page includes a detailed size chart. We recommend measuring your chest, waist, and hips, then comparing to the chart. When in doubt, size up.
Do you ship across India?
Yes! We ship pan-India via premium courier services. Orders above ₹999 enjoy free shipping. Delivery typically takes 3–7 business days.
Are the products authentic?
Absolutely. Every product at Your Wonderland is 100% authentic, quality-checked, and sourced from verified manufacturers and designers.

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect your name, email address, shipping address, and payment information when you create an account or make a purchase.

Your information is used to process orders, communicate about your purchases, and improve our services. We do not sell your personal data.
